Data Description

With the description of our data we are now moving from knowledge organization to knowledge representation. Based on the realized conceptual model, the description will focus on the central entities of our domain, using both predicates and natural language. In order to do that, we produced a series of triplets-based tables, expressing in the heading the subject of the description and in the columns the related predicates and objects. The column named question clarifies, in natural language, the meaning of the chosen predicate.
