RDF

In this final step data are described using a RDF statement, that we decided to serialize through Turtle. URIs have been minted for every entity described. In addition, we connected our data to other related items and resources on the World Wide Web by using different types of semantic associations.

We decided to describe three selected and different entities, out of the seven most important of our project, with the aim of representing how connections work between people, places and events. The entities are: the Hungarian coronation, a biographical event, Elisabeth of Austria, one of the protagonist of the event, and the Royal palace of Gödöllő , a place to the other entities.  A graphical representation of the RDF statement is also provided.
